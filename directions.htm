Directions:
 
Coming on Moreland at Ponce de Leon
Heading south, after passing across Ponce de leon Ave, drive about .5 mile more passing through 3 traffic lights (North Ave, Fredom Pkwy and Mansfield Ave) and make a left turn into the driveway between Wish and the Star Bar (there's a giant skull on your right...really).  Park in the rear and enter under the "427 Offices" green awning, take elevator ro second floor, Suite 200.  
 
        
 
From I-75/85
I-75/85to Exit 248  Freedom Parkway. Stay to the right, after you go under a large concrete tunnel-like structure, take the fork to the right. The sign at the split will say EAST 42. Follow the signs to EAST 42. The road ends at Moreland where you’ll turn right.  Go through one traffic light (Mansfield Ave) and make a left turn into the driveway between Wish and the Star Bar (there's a giant skull on your right...really).  Park in the rear and enter under the "427 Offices" green awning, take elevator ro second floor, Suite 200.    
 
     
 
From I-20
Exit 60 at Moreland Ave, head north about 1.4 miles.  You will pass Zesto on your right at McLendon Ave traffic light, make a right at the next almost immediate traffic light onto Euclid Ave, turn left into the first parking lot.  Park and enter under the "427 Offices" green awning, take elevator ro second floor, Suite 200.
