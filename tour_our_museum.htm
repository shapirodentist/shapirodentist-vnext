Dr. Shapiro began collecting Dental Antiques after receiving a graduation gift of several old Dental books. Since then he has amassed several hundred items which are on display in his office.
 
The collection primarily spans from the early 1800's to the early 1900's, and contains a foot powered dental drill, books, chairs, instruments, pictures, ads, lights, signs, sterilizers, business cards, cabinets, charts, diplomas, x-ray, electrical panel, lab equipment, traveling Dentist kits, dental office equipment, toothbrushes, a large collection of toothpastes and mouthwashes, artificial teeth, dental quackery items, dental statuettes, supplies, etc.
